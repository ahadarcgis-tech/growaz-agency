Our Story

A Mother & Daughter's Kitchen Legacy

Daughter Spoon began as a cherished tradition — a mother teaching her daughter the art of home cooking. What started with family recipes whispered over simmering pots has blossomed into a labor of love, bringing the warmth and soul of homemade food to our community.

Every dish we prepare carries the spirit of those early kitchen memories. We use only the freshest local ingredients, traditional spice blends passed down through generations, and the kind of care you can taste in every single bite.
